Cinnamon Roll Wreath with Apples

Ingredients

  • Crescent roll dough
  • Granulated sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Unsalted butter
  • Pazazz apples
  • Cream cheese
  • Milk
  • Vanilla extract
  • Powdered sugar

Instructions

  1. Roll out the crescent roll dough.
  2. Top with the cinnamon sugar butter.
  3. Add the apples.
  4. Roll it up into a tube.
  5. Slice it in half.
  6. Separate the two halves.
  7. Braid them together.
  8. Form the wreath and bake at 350Â°F for 20-25 minutes.
